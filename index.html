Gelato and World

Gelato and World was born out of my passion for traveling with the aim of inspiring other people to see the world and do everything they love. After traveling through 21 countries, while studying and working full time, my aim is to show others that traveling is not just a distant dream.

On this site, you will find tips, city guides, travel inspiration and stories of my adventures around the world. Furthermore, you will discover how to make travel a priority to ensure a life full of amazing experiences!
